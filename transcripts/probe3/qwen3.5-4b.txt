**Inductive Step:** Assume $a(k) = 2^{k+1} - 1$ for some $k \ge 1$.

### Method 2: Solving the Recurrence Relation

The recurrence is $a(n) - 2a(n-1) = 1$.
This is a linear non-homogeneous recurrence relation.
